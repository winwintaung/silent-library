Privacy Policy – Silent Library Website

Effective Date: 1 July 2025
Last Updated: 1 July 2025

At Silent Library, we are committed to protecting the personal information of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard any information you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information (via forms):
    Name, Email address, Contact number, Messages or inquiries sent through the contact form
  • Non-Personal Information:
    Browser type and version, Device type, IP address, Pages visited and time spent, Cookies and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries or feedback
  • To improve website functionality and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and performance (via analytics tools)
  • To send occasional updates or event invitations, if you opt in

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or government authorities
  • To protect the rights and safety of Silent Library or its users
  • If you provide explicit consent to share your information

4. Data Security

  • All form submissions are secured using HTTPS
  • Limited access is granted only to authorized library personnel
  • Regular updates and monitoring are in place to protect against unauthorized access

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

  • Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and track site usage
  • You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., National Library Board, external reading platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Please review their respective privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw your consent for communication
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal limitations)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@silentlibrary.sg.
